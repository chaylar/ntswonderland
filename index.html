Art Gallery of Nova Scotia, AGNS

Posted 45 minutes ago

Located in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada, the Art Gallery of Nova Scotia (AGNS) is a provincial public art museum.The museum was established in 1908 as the Nova Scotia Museum of Fine Arts and was renamed the Art Gallery of Nova Scotia in 1975.

Halifax Waterfront

Posted 45 minutes ago

The vibrant Waterfront neighbourhood is well-known for its revamped warehouses that now have cafés and stores, as well as its strollable boardwalk dotted with seafood eateries. The Halifax Seaport Farmers' Market features regional products including maple syrup and cranberry preserves, and the neighbourhood is home to a number of establishments, including the Canadian Museum of Immigration. Traditional pubs have live folk, blues, and rock performances at night.
