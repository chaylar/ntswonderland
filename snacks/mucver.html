Traditional Turkish Zucchini Fritters, Mücver

Snacks

Ingredients

  • 3 zucchini
  • 1 carrot
  • 6 pieces of green onion
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1,5 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • ¾ cup crushed walnuts
  • ½ cup cream cheese

Hey everyone! Welcome to Nt’s Wonderland kitchen corner! Today, I’m sharing a perfect movie night snack: Traditional Turkish Zucchini Fritters, also known as Mücver.

  1. Wash thoroughly 3 zucchini because we are not going to peel them. Then grate them all and squeeze until there is no water in them.
  2. If you would like to make it for a group of people it is better to grate a couple of potatoes as well and mix it with zucchini.
  3. Grate 1 carrot, chopped 6 pieces of green onion, mix them with zucchini and 2 eggs.
  4. Add 1 cup of flour, 1,5 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon black pepper
  5. I personally love to add ¾ cup crushed walnuts, and ½ cup cream cheese. Mix well with the spatula.
  6. Get ½ cup of that mix and fry them one by one.
